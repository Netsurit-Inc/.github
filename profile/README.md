# Netsurit, Inc

> Internal DX tooling and automation supporting Netsurit's mission to deliver reliable technology, robust security, and continuous innovation.

## About Netsurit

[Netsurit](https://netsurit.com/en-us/) is a global managed services provider helping organizations tackle modern workplace challenges through end-to-end IT expertise. We ensure business-critical applications and infrastructure are always on, secure, and resilient.

With headquarters in New York and Johannesburg, we deliver comprehensive managed services including:
- 24/7 security operations and threat detection
- Cloud optimization and migration
- AI and automation implementation
- Digital transformation consulting
- Enterprise infrastructure management

## What We Build

Our development organization builds the internal tooling and automation that powers Netsurit's service delivery:

- **Developer Experience (DX) Tools** - CLI utilities, templates, and workflows that enable our engineering teams to move fast and maintain quality
- **Automation Platforms** - Infrastructure-as-code, deployment pipelines, and orchestration systems that scale our managed services
- **Internal APIs & Integrations** - Systems that connect our monitoring, ticketing, and security platforms into cohesive workflows
- **Innovation Lab Projects** - Experimental tools leveraging AI, machine learning, and emerging technologies to improve service delivery

## Our Culture

At Netsurit, we believe in **"Supporting the dreams of the doers."** This philosophy extends to both our clients and our team:
