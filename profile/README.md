## About Netsurit

We're the internal development team at [Netsurit](https://netsurit.com/en-us/), a global managed services provider helping organizations tackle modern workplace challenges through end-to-end IT expertise. With headquarters in New York and Johannesburg, we ensure business-critical applications and infrastructure are always on, secure, and resilient.

Our comprehensive managed services include:
- 24/7 security operations and threat detection
- Cloud optimization and migration
- AI and automation implementation
- Digital transformation consulting
- Enterprise infrastructure management

We've been recognized as a Top 501 Global MSP since 2009 and have won the Global MSP Mentor award 7 times.

## What We Build

Our development organization creates the internal tooling and automation that powers Netsurit's service delivery:

**Developer Experience Tools** – CLI utilities, project templates, and workflows that enable our engineering teams to move fast and maintain quality

**Automation & Orchestration** – Infrastructure-as-code, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment systems that scale our managed services operations

**Integration Layer** – APIs and connectors that unify our monitoring, ticketing, and security platforms into cohesive workflows

**Innovation Lab** – Experimental projects leveraging AI, and emerging technologies to enhance service delivery

## Our Culture

At Netsurit, we live by **"Supporting the dreams of the doers."** This philosophy shapes how we work with both our clients and our team.

We value:
- **Respect, loyalty, and honesty** in all interactions
- **Clear and considerate** communication
- **Active mentorship and growth** for everyone on the team
- **Work-life integration** through our Dreams Programme, which encourages team members to set and pursue personal goals

Our approach to development:
- Build with empathy for the people who use our tools
- Automate thoughtfully—what should be automated should work flawlessly
- Stay curious and adapt as technology evolves
- Collaborate openly to find the best solutions
